# Bird Care Guidance

## Feeding
Birds benefit from consistent daily feeding and water refresh routines. Rapid behavior or appetite changes should be handled carefully.

## Enrichment
Birds need stimulation such as foraging, toys, and interaction. A schedule that lacks enrichment may not support long-term wellbeing.

## Cleaning
Cage cleaning and habitat upkeep are important recurring care tasks. These routines help maintain a healthier environment.

## Safety
Bird care guidance should stay cautious around illness, breathing issues, and environmental hazards. The app should direct urgent cases to a veterinarian.
