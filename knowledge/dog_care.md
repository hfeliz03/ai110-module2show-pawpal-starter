# Dog Care Guidance

## Exercise
Dogs usually benefit from daily movement and mental stimulation. A healthy adult dog often needs at least one walk or active play session each day. If a schedule has only feeding tasks and no walking or play, the care plan may be incomplete.

## Feeding
Dogs do best with predictable feeding routines. Feeding tasks should be clearly scheduled, and major changes in appetite or drinking should be monitored and discussed with a veterinarian.

## Enrichment
Play, sniffing, puzzle toys, and training sessions help prevent boredom. A balanced plan should include enrichment in addition to basic feeding and bathroom routines.

## Medication
Medication tasks should stay on a consistent schedule. The planner can remind the owner, but the app should not give diagnosis or dose changes.

## Senior Care
Older dogs may need shorter sessions, more breaks, and close attention to mobility changes. Recommendations should stay cautious and encourage veterinary input for health concerns.
