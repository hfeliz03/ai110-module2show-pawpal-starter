# Cat Care Guidance

## Feeding
Cats often thrive on a consistent feeding routine. Regular meal timing can reduce stress and make it easier to notice appetite changes.

## Enrichment
Indoor cats benefit from daily play and stimulation. Wand toys, climbing, scratching, and short interactive sessions can support physical and mental health.

## Grooming
Some cats need brushing support, especially long-haired cats. Grooming tasks can help reduce mats and hairballs when done consistently.

## Medication
Medication reminders are useful, but the system should avoid replacing veterinary advice. Urgent symptoms or missed critical doses should be escalated to a professional.

## Senior Care
Older cats may need lower-impact routines and closer monitoring for appetite, hydration, or litter box behavior changes.
