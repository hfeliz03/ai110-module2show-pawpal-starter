# Rabbit Care Guidance

## Feeding
Rabbits need consistent feeding habits with hay as a major part of daily care. Sudden appetite changes can be serious and should not be handled only by the app.

## Enrichment
Rabbits need time for exploration, chewing-safe toys, and gentle interaction. A plan with only feeding tasks may miss important enrichment needs.

## Grooming
Regular grooming can help manage shedding and reduce fur ingestion. Grooming frequency depends on coat and season.

## Safety
Rabbit routines should avoid excessive stress, heat exposure, and unsafe environments. The app should remain conservative when guidance is limited.
